Description

The Razer Cobra Pro is a wireless gaming mouse designed for precision, speed, and customization. Equipped with the Focus Pro 30K sensor and HyperSpeed wireless, it delivers lightning-fast response with flawless tracking. Its 58g ultra-light design and ergonomic shape make it ideal for competitive play, while 10-zone RGB lighting adds a touch of style. With Gen-3 Optical Switches and long battery life, the Cobra Pro is built to dominate in every game session.
