Epson EcoTank ET-1810

Specifications

  • Print technology: Inkjet, 4-colour (CMYK)
  • Print resolution: Up to 5760 x 1440 dpi
  • Connectivity: USB, Wi-Fi, Wi-Fi Direct
  • Print speed: Up to 33 pages per minute (black)
  • Borderless photo printing (up to A4)
  • Ultra-low-cost printing – up to 4,500 pages (black) / 7,500 pages (colour)
  • Refillable ink tank system
  • Compatible with Windows, macOS, iOS, Android

Description

The Epson EcoTank ET-1810 is a compact, wireless colour printer ideal for everyday home and small office printing. Featuring Epson’s revolutionary cartridge-free ink tank system, the ET-1810 reduces running costs while delivering sharp, vibrant prints. With easy Wi-Fi setup
